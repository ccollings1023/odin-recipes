Easy Homemade Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a super easy and super yummy recipe for making your own pasta at home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 2 Tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 Tablespoons water, or as needed

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Mix flour, eggs, olive oil, and salt in a bowl untill combined. Add water, 1 teaspoon at a time, to flour mixture until a smooth, thick dough forms.
  3. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured work surface and knead for 10 minutes. Let dough rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Divide dough into eight balls; us a pasta machine to roll and cut dough into desired pasta shape.
  5. Boil in salted water for approx two minutes, then serve
