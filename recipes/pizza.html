Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a TASTY and easy recipe perfect for a gathering or night in.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1.5 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1.125 cups warm water 110-115 degrees F
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • .5 cup corn oil
  • 1.5 teaspoons kosher salt
  • butter as needed, for greasing

Steps

  1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Combine yeast mixture, flour, corn oil, and kosher salt in a large stand mixer fitted with the dough hook; knead until dough holds together but is still sticky, about 2 minutes.
  3. Form dough into ball and transfer to a buttered bowl. Turn to coat dough with butter, then cover the bowl with a towel and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 6 hours.
  4. Punch down dough and let the rest for 10 to 15 minutes. Press Dough into a 10-inch deep-dish pizza pan and follow your pizza recipe.

To Make the Pizza:

Add cheese, toppings, and tomatoes that you've flavored with garlic, basil, oregano, etc. Bake the pizza in an oven preheated to 450 degrees F for about 30 minutes, depending on your oven. you can prebake the crust for 10-15 minutes befor adding toppings if desired.
